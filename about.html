Connecting Engineering & Medicine

About IEEE EMBS Vardhaman

Engineering the future of healthcare through technology, research, student innovation, and global collaboration.

Our Mission

To advance engineering concepts and science for the benefit of human health by training students in biomedical technologies, telemetry, wearable health sensors, and AI-driven clinical tools.

Our Vision

To establish Vardhaman College of Engineering as a premier hub for biomedical engineering research, interdisciplinary innovation, and industry-ready healthcare engineering graduates.

Core Objectives

  • Organize hands-on biomedical signal processing workshops
  • Connect students with IEEE EMBS global experts
  • Support student research papers & patents
