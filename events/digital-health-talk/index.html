About the Session

The IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (IEEE EMBS) Student Branch Chapter, Vardhaman College of Engineering, is organising an expert talk on "Digital Health, Telemedicine, and Remote Patient Monitoring Systems."

This session provides insights into the latest advancements in digital healthcare technologies, telemedicine platforms, remote patient monitoring, wearable health devices, AI in healthcare, and the future of connected medical services.

Who Should Attend?
UG & PG Students Faculty Members IEEE & Non-IEEE Members Researchers

Registration Deadline: 12 August 2026, 11:59 PM IST

Topics to be Covered

Digital Health

Introduction to the modern digital health landscape — platforms, ecosystems, and policy.

Telemedicine

Evolution and real-world applications of telemedicine in clinical practice today.

Remote Monitoring

Technologies enabling continuous Remote Patient Monitoring and real-time vitals.

IoT & Wearables

IoT sensors and wearable devices transforming everyday patient health tracking.

AI & Analytics

AI and Data Analytics powering faster, smarter clinical decision-making.

Future Trends & Security

Challenges, data security, privacy regulations and the next frontier in digital health.

Meet the Speaker

Dr. Ajit Kumar, Ph.D.

Associate Professor, Information Systems

Xavier Institute of Management (XIM University), Bhubaneswar, Odisha

Dr. Ajit Kumar holds a Ph.D. from Taiwan and specialises in Information Systems, Digital Health, and AI-driven healthcare technologies. With extensive research in telemedicine platforms, data analytics, and smart healthcare ecosystems, he brings a rich blend of academic expertise and industry insights. He has published in leading journals and delivered talks at international forums on the future of connected healthcare.
