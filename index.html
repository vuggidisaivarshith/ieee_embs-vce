About IEEE EMBS Vardhaman

The IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBS) Student Branch Chapter at Vardhaman College of Engineering is dedicated to bridging the gap between engineering and medicine. We aim to inspire and empower students to apply engineering principles to healthcare challenges.

Through hands-on workshops, expert talks, and collaborative projects, we provide our members with the skills and knowledge needed to innovate in the rapidly evolving fields of medical technology.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be an IEEE member to attend events?

No! Our expert talks and general seminars are open to all students, faculty, and researchers across all engineering departments. IEEE membership gives you additional perks like travel grants and conference discounts.

How are E-Certificates issued to attendees?

E-certificates are generated and dispatched via email after attendance verification for participants who attend the full live session. You can also verify your certificate anytime on our Certificate Verification Portal.

How do I join the IEEE EMBS Executive Committee?

We conduct annual recruitment drives for executive committee roles (design, web, event management, public relations). Submit your interest via our Join Us page or reach out to the Secretary directly.
